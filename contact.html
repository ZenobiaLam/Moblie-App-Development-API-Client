 常見問題 Frequently Asked Questions 如何使用本應用程式？ How to use this application?

您可以在首頁使用搜尋欄或分類選單來找到適合您的瑜伽動作。點擊任何動作項目可查看詳細資訊、示範圖片和教學影片。

You can use the search bar or category menus on the home page to find yoga poses suitable for you. Click on any pose item to view detailed information, demonstration images, and tutorial videos.

如何選擇適合自己的瑜伽動作？ How to choose suitable yoga poses?

初學者建議從「初級」難度的動作開始練習。您也可以根據自己的需求（如增強力量、提升柔軟度等）使用分類功能找到適合的動作。請注意每個動作的注意事項，避免造成傷害。

Beginners are recommended to start with "Beginner" level poses. You can also use the categorization feature to find suitable poses based on your needs (such as strengthening, improving flexibility, etc.). Please pay attention to the cautions for each pose to avoid injury.

我可以建議新的瑜伽動作嗎？ Can I suggest new yoga poses?

當然可以！請使用聯絡表單，告訴我們您希望增加的瑜伽動作資訊，我們會盡快回應並考慮加入。

Of course! Please use the contact form to tell us about the yoga pose information you would like to add, and we will respond as soon as possible and consider adding it.
